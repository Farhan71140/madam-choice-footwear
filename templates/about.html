About Us

Madam Choice Footwear was established in 2005 by Md. Siraj Uddin. For two decades, we’ve proudly served the Secunderabad community from our shop at # B-13, Palika Bazar, Station Road, Secunderabad – 500 003.

We specialize in ladies footwear — offering a wide variety of styles including comfortable daily wear flats, elegant pencil heels, trendy wedges, flip-flops, shoes, and fancy wear items. Our goal is to combine quality, affordability, and fashion to meet every customer’s need.

This website is proudly owned & managed by Md. Siraj Uddin, ensuring our digital presence reflects the same care and quality as our footwear.
